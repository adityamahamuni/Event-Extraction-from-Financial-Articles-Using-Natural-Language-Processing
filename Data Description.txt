EDT Dataset
The EDT dataset is created to support corporate event detection and news-based stock prediction. It includes:

9721 News Articles: Annotated with token-level event labels.
303893 News Articles: With minute-level timestamps and comprehensive stock price labels.

Event Types
The paper focuses on 11 types of corporate events that have predictable impacts on stock prices:

Guidance Increase
Acquisition
New Contract
Stock Split
Reverse Stock Split
Positive Clinical Trial & FDA Approval
Stock Repurchase
Dividend
Dividend Cut
Dividend Increase
Special Dividend
